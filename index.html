In a few words

I am a biologist by training with a solid experience in image processing and data analysis. During my career, I notably studied how actin filaments dynamics could influence larger scale processes in various in vivo and in vitro models. My research has allowed me to develop a keen interest in quantifying complex processes through microscopy image analysis. This encouraged me to join and help setting up a multi-disciplinary platform that supports researchers and students from the CENTURI scientific community .
